SequenceAlignX is an educational web application that demonstrates local sequence alignment in bioinformatics. It provides a stepwise filling of the alignment matrix, visual tracing of optimal paths, and an optional 'Guess Mode' where users predict alignment steps. The tool helps students and researchers understand alignment algorithms interactively.
