Pediatric Radiograph Diagnosis is a specialized deep learning project aimed at detecting pneumonia in pediatric chest X-rays. It employs a fine-tuned ResNet-50 model with a two-stage training strategy to maximize feature extraction and classification accuracy. The system achieves a high sensitivity of 93.6%, critical for medical screening. A key feature is the integration of Grad-CAM, which provides visual heatmaps to explain the model's diagnostic decisions, enhancing trust and clinical utility.
