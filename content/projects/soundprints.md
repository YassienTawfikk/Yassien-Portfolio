Soundprints is an advanced desktop application for audio recognition, combining spectrogram-based feature extraction, perceptual hashing, and similarity analysis. Users can identify songs, vocals, or music from a pre-saved database, mix audio files, and analyze similarity scores efficiently. The system is optimized for educational, research, and practical applications in audio signal processing.
