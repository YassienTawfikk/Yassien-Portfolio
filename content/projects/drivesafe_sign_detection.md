DriveSafe-Sign-Detection is a traffic sign recognition system developed with a custom CNN and MobileNetV2. Trained on the GTSRB dataset, the project achieves high accuracy in classifying 43 types of German traffic signs. The system includes modular components for data preprocessing, model training, evaluation, and visualization. Comparative analysis highlights the superior performance of the CNN over a frozen MobileNetV2 baseline, emphasizing end-to-end learning on task-specific features. Ideal for research, ADAS prototyping, and educational purposes.
